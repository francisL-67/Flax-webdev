Audwin Jalen Gray also known as Oddwin

is a rapper, singer, and songwriter from Richland Mississippi.

He is known for his unique style and sound, which blends elements of hip-hop, R&B, and pop music. Oddwin has released several successful albums and singles, and has gained a large following on social media.

He is also known for his energetic live performances

and has toured extensively throughout the United States and internationally. Oddwin is considered one of the rising stars in the music industry and has been praised for his creativity and originality.

On May 3, 2023, Oddwin released his biggest song to date, KRUSTY KREW ANTHEM (BACK ON THE GRILL) by KASH KRABS,

a character based off Mr. Krabs from the popular cartoon SpongeBob SquarePants. The song would become a viral hit, garnering tens of millions of streams across all platforms.

On January 19, 2024, Oddwin released his first album post Kash Krabs, jus hav fun (vol. 2).

It featured big songs like “LOOK AT ME NOW” and “WARIO & WALUIGI,” further proving that Oddwin was much more than just a joke artist and didn’t need to rely on gimmicks to be successful.

Outside of music, Oddwin is also a streamer and content creator. He gained initial success through various viewer submission videos, most notably his troll beats series where viewers could submit purposely funny/bad beats for him to react and try not to laugh at.
